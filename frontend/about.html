About Unifina Group

Egypt's trusted partner in finance and insurance since 2012

Who We Are

Unifina Group is a leading Egyptian financial services company headquartered in Maadi, Cairo. Since our founding in 2012, we have grown to serve over 10,000 clients across Egypt through our four core business lines: Investment Management, Home Financing, Equipment Leasing, and Insurance.

Our Mission

To provide smart, accessible, and trustworthy financial solutions that empower individuals and businesses to achieve their goals. We combine deep local expertise with international standards to deliver results that matter.

Our Business Lines

We operate across four specialized divisions — Investment Management for portfolio growth, Home Financing for property dreams, Equipment Leasing for business expansion, and Insurance for comprehensive protection. Each division is staffed by certified professionals dedicated to your financial success.
